faq

Do you handle the final development, or strictly the design and strategy?

I specialize exclusively in the design and strategy phase. Like an architect provides the technical drawings for a contractor, I deliver the design system and assets that your developers will use to build a pixel-perfect site. This focus ensures your brand identity is never compromised by technical limitations.

How does your design approach directly help my firm attract high-value clients?

I eliminate the visual gap between your real-world landmarks and your digital presence. This establishes the immediate, undeniable authority required to secure high-ticket contracts before a client even jumps on a call.

What is required from my firm to begin the identity translation process?

To reflect your caliber, we need your highest-quality project photography and a clear understanding of your firm’s core values. If you have established brand guidelines, I work within them; if not, we define the visual language during Phase 01 to ensure the interface truly meets your identity.

Can you work within our firm’s existing brand standards?

Absolutely. Many high-end firms have established identities they want to protect. I specialize in translating your existing physical brand into a digital environment, ensuring your online presence feels like a seamless extension of your firm’s culture and work.

What is the typical timeline and investment for a premium design project?

A standard engagement usually spans 3 to 8 weeks, depending on the complexity of your portfolio and structural needs. Since every firm’s identity is unique, investment is based on the strategic scope required to turn your website into a high-performance growth asset.
